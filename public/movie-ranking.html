The best Tv Shows according to Danis

My top 3 best TV shows of all time

House of Cards

House of Cards is a TV show on Netflix that features Kevin Spacey as the main actor playing the character of Francis Underwood, corrupt and evil politician whose only goal is power.
"Power is just like real estate, its all about location, location, location"

Succession

Succession is a TV show on HBO Max that is about a billionaire family. The company is ran by the father, Logan Roy he is the one who made it all, but with his health deteriorating someone has to take the wheel instead. He is not keen to that fact and is trying to keep it all under himself. His son Kendall is the one who was supposed to succeed his father is not very happy about the fact that his father doesnt want to retire.
"Money Wins"

The Suits

The Suits is a show about 2 lawyers. One of them is the best closing lawyer in NYC and the other one is a dropout drug dealer with photographic memory. They become very close and start working together, keeping their secret together. They win cases together and try to keep eachother out of jail because of their secret of the one of them not being a real lawyer
"If I Hit This Button, I Call Michael Jordan."
